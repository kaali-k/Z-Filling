# Z Filling – Puzzle Arcade Game 🍬

**Z Filling** is an addictive, fast-paced puzzle arcade game for Android. Challenge your spatial reasoning as you fill grids, earn sweet rewards, and unlock new tiers in this minimalist game experience.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay Features

- **🧠 Strategic Grids**: Fill the patterns perfectly to maximize your score.
- **🍭 Sweet Tiers**: Progress through levels to reach the highest "Sweet Tiers".
- **🕹️ Smooth Mechanics**: Optimized for quick sessions—perfect for playing on the go.
- **🎨 Minimalist Design**: Clean, distracting-free visuals that focus entirely on the puzzle.
- **🏆 High Score Tracker**: Challenge yourself and keep track of your best runs locally.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Framework**: [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/) (Flame-like logic with Custom Painters)
- **Language**: Dart
- **Platform**: Android
